About the UCC

The United Corgi Commonwealth (UCC) is a federation of the Empire of Pembroke and the Republic of Cardigan. Founded to secure justice, biscuits, and the blessings of Pawgod/Dawgod for all Corgis.

"Corgito Ergo Sum" (I bark therefore I am).

The legendary Bone of Contention, sacred relic of our shared heritage.

Our Governance

The Senate & Consuls

Senate of 201 Corgis, champions of the Great Games. Two annually elected Consuls—one Pembroke, one Cardigan—lead executive matters.

"All power originates from every Corgi, individually and collectively."

Constitutional Principles

Rights: freedom of bark, tail movement, pursuit of squirrels. Exile is the harshest penalty; harmony is our goal.

Decisions proclaimed by "The bones have been cast" (Kości zostały rzucone), followed by a unified "Woof!"
